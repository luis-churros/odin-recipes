Pastel recipe

About

One of my favorites food, A pastel is a typical Brazilian fast-food dish, consisting of half-circle or rectangle-shaped thin crust pies with assorted fillings, fried in oil.

The result is a crispy, brownish fried pie.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients in a bowl and knead with your hands.
  2. Knead the dough well on a floured, smooth surface.
  3. Use a rolling pin to stretch the dough to the desire thickness
  4. Cut the dough to the size you want the pasteis
  5. fill according to your preference
  6. Fry in hot oil
  7. Enjoy!
